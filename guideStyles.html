3. Common Encoded URLS

Original URL:

https://gojak.com/query=hello world

Encoded URL:

https://gojak.com/query=hello%20world

Some common encoded characters:

  • Space → %20
  • & → %26
  • = → %3D
  • ? → %3F

4. Guide Style (HTML Best Practices)

Messy HTML:

<DIV><IMG src=cat.jpg><h1>Welcome</h1></DIV>

Fixed HTML:

<div>
    <img src="cat.jpg" alt="A cute cat">
    <h1>Welcome</h1>
</div>
